Find the cheapest fuel and EV chargers around you in 17 countries — real-time, free, no account, no ads. Independent and open-source.

WHY THIS APP
• Real-time prices from official open-data partners — not crowdsourced guesses.
• 17 countries supported, with one unified search that mixes petrol, diesel, LPG, CNG, e85 and EV charging stations on the same map.
• Built for drivers who don't want to think about it: pair your OBD2 adapter once and trips record themselves.
• Privacy-first: no PII leaves your device, no online inference, full data deletion in one tap.

CORE FEATURES
• Search by current location, address, or favourite radius — sorted by best price for your fuel type.
• Map view with the cheapest pin highlighted, plus full station detail (opening hours, brand, payment, distance).
• 30-day local price history with a "best time to fill" predictor and holiday-aware adjustments.
• Price alerts: single station or radius, with consent-respecting evaluators that run only when you're nearby.
• Favourites, cross-border savings hint (e.g. FR → DE / DE → CZ), home-screen widget, voice announcement during driving.

DRIVING
• OBD2 trip recording — pair an ELM327-compatible adapter once and the app records every trip end to end.
• Hands-free auto-record (optional): trips start when the engine spins up and save themselves on disconnect. A launcher badge tells you new auto-recordings are waiting.
• Driving insights with hard-acceleration markers on the trip-detail map, RPM/throttle histograms, eco-coach hints.
• Consumption tracker, maintenance reminders, full backup/restore.

SUPPORTED COUNTRIES (real-time data)
Austria, Argentina, Australia, Chile, Denmark, France, Germany, Greece, Italy, Luxembourg, Mexico, Portugal, Romania, Slovenia, South Korea, Spain, United Kingdom.

EV CHARGING
Open Charge Map integration — every public charger surfaces alongside fuel pumps in the unified search results, with connector/power info on the detail screen.

LANGUAGES
23 UI languages: Bulgarian, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Estonian, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Hungarian, Italian, Latvian, Lithuanian, Norwegian Bokmål, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swedish.

GMS-FREE
A separate F-Droid build is available with no Google Mobile Services dependency, for users who run de-Googled Android variants.
